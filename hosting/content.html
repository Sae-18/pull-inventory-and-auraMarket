About the Contest

The Creator's Contest is the ultimate trial - a once-in-a-lifetime battle where skill, strategy, and ego are pushed beyond their limits. The Creator does not stand alone. Guarding him are his Five Swords - legendary warriors who test the strength of every challenger. Each Sword wields exclusive, custom-forged cards, crafted to break the will of ordinary players. Only those with unshakable resolve can hope to cut through their defenses.

The Challenger's Path

Steps to Enter the Contest

  1. 1

    Become a Tournament Champion in any TCG on our platform (paid community).

  2. 2

    Face the Five Swords, one by one. Each Sword wields exclusive cards, but you may also choose from special cards to aid you.

  3. 3

    Defeat all the Swords to earn the right to face the Creator himself.

  4. 4

    Conquer the Creator, and claim your destiny.
